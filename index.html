About Us

Welcome to Asian Cafe Concord!

Explore the Flavors of China, Sushi, Thailand, Vietnam & Hibachi

Asian Cafe is a family-owned and operated restaurant with a passion for bringing people together over delicious food. Each distinctive dish is handcrafted to order and prepared with only the finest and freshest ingredients.

Our combination of traditional Asian cooking techniques allows us to create exceptional pan-Asian cuisine. From our create-your-own stir fry bar and award-winning sushi bar to our warm, family-friendly atmosphere, we strive to make every dining experience unforgettable.

We treasure our customers as an extension of our family and are thrilled to share our love for food with you. At Asian Cafe, you'll always discover more than you expect—a heartfelt welcome, exceptional dishes, and a place where families and friends create lasting memories.
